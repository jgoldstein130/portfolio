Freshlink

Freshlink was a web app that I developed with other students at the University of Kansas. I worked with two other developers, a business student, and a graphic designer. The goal of FreshLink was to be a website where students at the University of Kansas could go to learn more about campus organizations and meet like-minded students. The web app supported features such as user look-up, media feeds, content creation, and profile pages. The technologies used were Vue JS, HTML, CSS, and Google Firebase.
